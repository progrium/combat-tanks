Combat Tanks Alpha

This is an early version of a multiplayer Flash game I'm working on called Combat Tanks. It's a remake of Michael Morrison's Combat Tanks, which itself was a remake of the tank mode of the the Atari 2600 game Combat. I really enjoyed playing Combat Tanks with my dad, but the game is fairly inaccessible now. I also thought it would benefit from networked multiplayer and my brand of crappy pixel art.
